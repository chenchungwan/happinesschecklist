Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This app stores your entries in Apple’s iCloud via Core Data + CloudKit so your data stays within your Apple account. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

Data we store

  • Daily notes you enter for Gratitude, Kindness, Connection, Meditation, and Savoring.
  • Basic app settings (e.g., onboarding status) stored locally on device.

Data access

Your iCloud account controls access to your synced data. Removing the app or using the in‑app “Delete All Data” will remove your entries from your private CloudKit database.
